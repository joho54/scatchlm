Chapter 3: Verb Conjugation

The present tense in English is used to describe habitual actions, general truths, and states of being.

Regular verbs follow a predictable pattern:

I walk → He walks

I study → She studies

Exercise 3.1: Conjugate the following verbs in the present tense.

1. (to run) She _______

2. (to fly) The bird _______

3. (to carry) He _______

4. (to watch) They _______

5. (to go) She _______

Rule: For verbs ending in consonant + y, drop the y and add -ies.

fly → flies, carry → carries, study → studies

Exercise 3.2: Irregular verbs

to be → I am, you are, he is

to have → I have, she has

to do → I do, it does
